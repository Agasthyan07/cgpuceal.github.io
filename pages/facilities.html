Facilities

The training cell is a professional body of the college run by the skilled students of the college,
under the expert guidance of the staff. This body facilitates the process of placement of students passing out from the Institute
besides collaborating with leading organizations and institutes in the setting up of training programs for the students of this esteemed college.
The Training Cell's aim is to create an environment of technical and soft skill learning, to develop them in accordance with the current Industry standards.
